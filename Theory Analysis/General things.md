# **What we are building**


First of all, we are trying to build a distortion pedal for an electric guitar, specifically the Boss DS-1.

It is designed for hard and aggressive clipping, essentially cutting pieces of the signal that the guitar produces, making the output signal very distorted and metal-like.

We are going to use a 3PDT true bypass design for clean and transparent path for the bypassed signal


# **Circuit Parts**



The entire model consists of **5 different parts:**


- Input Buffer
	Present high impedance to the guitar preventing "tone sucking"

- Transition Booster
	Pre-amplifies the signal

- OP-Amp Gain Stage
	Generating the distortion effect

- Power Supply 
	Provides energy to all the circuit

- Tone level
	Tone filtering

- Output Buffer
	Present low impedance to the cables on the output preserving the signal over a long distance
